Hammer and Nail: AI in Healthcare

The first edition of HAN, a virtual event hosted by University of Catania and Messina

Description

The "Hammer and Nail: AI in Healthcare" event series is organized for master's and PhD students, aimed at connecting computer science students with startups, researchers, and industry professionals to foster innovation and collaboration in the field of healthcare AI. This series is designed for individuals engaged in machine learning and AI projects, seeking real-world applications and AI solutions to challenging problems. It facilitates communication and networking across different disciplines. The series promises to be a valuable experience, offering insights into the practical applications of AI in solving healthcare challenges.

Format

Each event features a presenter who discusses either a problem ("nail") in search of a solution ("hammer") or an existing solution seeking a relevant problem to address. The format includes a 30-minute presentation followed by a 30-minute question and discussion session, allowing attendees to discuss their work, gain valuable insights, explore potential collaborations, and stay updated on the latest AI solutions in healthcare.
